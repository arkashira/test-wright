# Marketing Plan for test-wright
## Positioning
### Primary Positioning Statement
"Automate testing with confidence: test-wright streamlines code verification for developers, saving time and reducing errors."

### Alternative Positioning Statements
* "Code confidence in seconds: test-wright's AI-powered testing tool revolutionizes software development."
* "Verify with ease: test-wright's automated testing solution empowers developers to focus on innovation."
* "Code with certainty: test-wright's AI-driven testing tool ensures quality and reliability."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Alex Chen, DevOps Engineer
* Daily Job: Lead automated testing for a mid-sized e-commerce platform
* Budget: $10,000 - $50,000 per year for testing tools and services
* Goals: Reduce testing time by 50%, improve code quality, and increase team efficiency

### Persona 2: Maya Patel, Software Development Manager
* Daily Job: Oversee software development for a large enterprise company
* Budget: $50,000 - $200,000 per year for testing tools and services
* Goals: Improve testing efficiency by 30%, reduce errors by 25%, and increase team productivity

### Persona 3: Ethan Kim, Freelance Developer
* Daily Job: Develop and deploy software applications for various clients
* Budget: $1,000 - $5,000 per year for testing tools and services
* Goals: Reduce testing time by 20%, improve code quality, and increase client satisfaction

## Channels
### Primary Channels
1. **Content Marketing**: Blog posts, case studies, and whitepapers on testing best practices and AI-powered testing solutions (CAC estimate: $50)
2. **Social Media**: LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook advertising targeting developers and development teams (CAC estimate: $75)
3. **Influencer Marketing**: Partner with industry influencers and thought leaders in software development and testing (CAC estimate: $100)

## Content Cadence
### Week 1-4: Awareness and Education
* Publish 2 blog posts on testing best practices and AI-powered testing solutions
* Share 2 social media posts on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook
* Engage with influencers and thought leaders in the industry

### Week 5-8: Product Showcase
* Publish 2 case studies on successful testing projects using test-wright
* Share 2 social media posts on product features and benefits
* Host a webinar on AI-powered testing solutions

### Week 9-12: Lead Generation
* Publish 2 whitepapers on testing best practices and AI-powered testing solutions
* Share 2 social media posts on lead magnets and free trials
* Run a social media contest for free trials and demos

## Launch Milestones
### D-30 (30 days before launch)
* Finalize marketing materials, including positioning statement, ICP, and channels
* Establish a content calendar and schedule content creation

### D-0 (launch day)
* Launch test-wright and announce it on social media and email marketing channels
* Host a launch webinar and Q&A session

### D+30 (30 days after launch)
* Analyze launch metrics and adjust marketing strategy as needed
* Publish a case study on a successful testing project using test-wright

### D+90 (90 days after launch)
* Publish a whitepaper on testing best practices and AI-powered testing solutions
* Share a social media post on $MRR target and DAU growth

## Success Metrics
### DAU (Daily Active Users)
* Target: 100 users by D+30, 500 users by D+90

### $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
* Target: $10,000 by D+30, $50,000 by D+90
