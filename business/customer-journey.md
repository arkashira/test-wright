# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer experiences delays or errors due to manual testing.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about automated testing tools, skepticism about AI capabilities.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, time pressure.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content on the benefits of AI-powered testing, case studies.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits, blog post views.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer searches for solutions to streamline testing processes.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options, confusion about tool capabilities.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, cautious.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison charts, demo videos, testimonials.
- **Metric**: Downloads of whitepapers, demo requests.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer signs up for a free trial or demo.
- **Friction Points**: Complex setup process, lack of immediate results.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful, anxious.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless onboarding, quick setup, immediate value demonstration.
- **Metric**: Trial sign-ups, completion rate of trial.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer experiences significant time savings and accuracy improvements.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing tools, cost concerns.
- **User Emotions**: Excited, relieved.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding, dedicated support, integration guides.
- **Metric**: Conversion to paid plans, retention rate.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer shares positive results with team or other teams.
- **Friction Points**: Scaling usage, additional feature needs.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfied, proactive.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Advanced feature access, team training, enterprise solutions.
- **Metric**: Additional feature adoption, team expansion.
